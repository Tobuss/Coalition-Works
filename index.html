Solivity

The Solivity series is my first series and novel, set in what I deem a Middle Fantasy setting. The book follows the adventures of a group of young Wolves as they are forced out of their home and into an adventure to try and prevent a war from errupting. I maintain a wiki with all of the most up to date information available in the current state of the series.
